Problem

What the analysis needed to answer

Identify customers likely to leave the bank and translate churn probability into practical retention priorities.

A retention-focused pipeline compares a balanced logistic baseline with a tuned random forest, then uses threshold planning and feature importance for action.

Findings

The signals that mattered most

  • Churn is imbalanced, so accuracy alone is not enough.
  • Age, activity status, product count, balance behavior, and geography are meaningful churn signals.
  • Estimated salary is weaker than account and behavioral features.
  • Threshold selection should depend on campaign cost and retention capacity.
Coverage

What the notebook covers

  • Segmentation by geography, gender, age band, activity, and products
  • Statistical churn tests and multicollinearity checks
  • Balanced logistic regression and tuned random forest
  • Feature importance, ROC, precision-recall, and threshold planning
